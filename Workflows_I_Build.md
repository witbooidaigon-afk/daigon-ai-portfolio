## How I build them

1. **Map the manual process** — what happens today, by hand, and where it's slow.
2. **Define the triggers, inputs, and outputs** before building.
3. **Build the happy path first**, then add branching for edge cases.
4. **Iterate in versions** and **harden for reliability** — retries, error handling, rate limits, timeouts.
5. **Document it** so it can be re-deployed to a new business quickly.
