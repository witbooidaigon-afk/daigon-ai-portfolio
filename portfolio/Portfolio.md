## 02 · About Daigon

Daigon (Dynamic Media) is an AI automation consultancy based in South Africa, serving clients locally and internationally. We install **revenue-protecting AI systems** for service businesses — voice agents, speed-to-lead, missed-call recovery, booking, outreach, and CRM automation — built on a self-hosted n8n platform orchestrating leading AI models and communication channels.

We position not as developers-for-hire, but as **operators who install infrastructure that makes our clients more money** while removing manual work.
