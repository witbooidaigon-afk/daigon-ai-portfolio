# Business Solutions

The same systems from the Workflow Library, translated into **products a business owner can buy** — with the problem, the before/after, the benefit, and an indicative price band.

## 1. AI Receptionist & Voice Agent
*"Never miss another call."*

- **Business problem:** Calls go unanswered after hours, during jobs, or at peak times — and each missed call is a lost customer.
- **Manual process today:** A receptionist (or the owner) answers when they can; the rest go to voicemail and are rarely returned.
- **Automated process:** An AI voice agent answers 24/7, qualifies the caller, answers common questions, books the job, and notifies the team.
- **Business benefits:** Captures after-hours and overflow calls; consistent, on-brand answering; no salary, sick days, or hold music.
- **Suitable industries:** Home services (plumbing, HVAC, electrical), clinics & spas, legal, real estate, automotive.
- **Indicative price:** **Setup $800–$2,500 (R15k–R46k)** + **$150–$500/mo (R2.7k–R9.2k)** retainer/usage.

## 2. Speed-to-Lead Response System
*"Reply in seconds, win the job."*

- **Business problem:** Leads buy from whoever responds first; manual follow-up takes hours.
- **Manual process today:** Someone eventually sees the form/ad lead and calls back — often too late.
- **Automated process:** New leads are contacted within seconds by SMS/WhatsApp/email, qualified, and routed to the team.
- **Business benefits:** Dramatically higher lead-to-booking conversion; no lead falls through the cracks.
- **Suitable industries:** Any business buying leads or running ads.
- **Indicative price:** **Setup $500–$1,500 (R9k–R27k)** + **$100–$300/mo (R1.8k–R5.5k)**.

## 3. Missed-Call Text-Back & No-Show Recovery
*"Turn missed calls into booked jobs."*

- **Business problem:** Missed calls and no-shows are silent revenue leaks.
- **Manual process today:** Missed calls are forgotten; no-shows are rarely rebooked.
- **Automated process:** Every missed call triggers an instant text-back; no-shows enter an automated recovery sequence.
- **Business benefits:** Recovers revenue with near-zero effort; the fastest-to-prove ROI in our catalogue.
- **Suitable industries:** Clinics, salons/spas, trades, restaurants, any appointment-based business.
- **Indicative price:** **Setup $300–$900 (R5.5k–R16k)** + **$80–$250/mo (R1.5k–R4.6k)**.

## 4. AI Appointment Booking System
