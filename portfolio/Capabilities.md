# Core Expertise & Capabilities

## At a Glance

Daigon operates at the intersection of **applied AI**, **business-process automation**, and **customer communications**. We translate a business problem ("we keep losing leads") into a deployed, monitored, always-on system — and maintain it.

---

## 1. AI Engineering Capabilities (Applied AI)

We engineer with AI; we do not train foundation models. Our strength is **orchestrating** state-of-the-art models into dependable business systems.

- **LLM Orchestration** — Multi-step reasoning chains across OpenAI (GPT-4o, GPT-4.1, GPT-5) and Anthropic Claude (Sonnet 4), with model selection tuned per task for cost and quality.
- **Conversational AI Agents** — Context-aware chat and messaging agents for sales, support, and booking across WhatsApp, web, Telegram, and SMS.
- **AI Voice Agents** — Inbound and outbound phone agents built on Retell AI and Vapi, with ElevenLabs voice and Whisper transcription, for real-time qualification, booking, and support.
- **Prompt Engineering & System Design** — Structured system prompts, guardrails, tool-calling, and fallback logic that keep agents on-brand and on-task.
- **Knowledge-Grounded Responses (RAG-style)** — Agents grounded in business-specific knowledge (services, pricing, FAQs, policies) so answers are accurate, not generic.
- **AI Document & Content Generation** — Automated proposals, quotes, outreach copy, and review requests generated from structured inputs.

## 2. Automation Engineering Capabilities

- **Workflow Orchestration** — Production **n8n** platform (self-hosted on a dedicated VPS) running 40+ workflows with webhooks, schedulers, branching logic, retries, and error handling.
- **Systems Integration** — Connecting CRMs, calendars, spreadsheets, messaging, and telephony into one coherent flow.
- **Event-Driven Triggers** — Form submissions, inbound calls, missed calls, new spreadsheet rows, webhooks, and time-based schedules.
- **Multi-Channel Messaging** — Coordinated SMS, WhatsApp, email, and Slack/Telegram notifications from a single source of truth.
- **Data Pipelines** — Reading, enriching, routing, and writing lead/customer data across Google Sheets, Airtable, Notion, and Postgres.

## 3. Customer-Communication & Revenue Systems

- **Speed-to-Lead** — Sub-minute response to inbound leads.
- **Missed-Call Recovery** — Automatic text-back and re-engagement.
- **Appointment Lifecycle** — Booking, rescheduling, cancellation, reminders, and no-show recovery via Cal.com and Google Calendar.
- **Outreach at Scale** — Personalised cold email/SMS sequences with reply detection and safe sending controls.
- **Reputation & Retention** — Automated review requests and follow-up nurture.

## 4. Delivery & Operations

- **Productized Builds** — Reusable system templates re-deployed to new clients quickly.
- **Deployment** — Self-hosted n8n on VPS (Hostinger), HTTP/webhook integrations, and managed credentials.
- **Maintenance & Monitoring** — Iterative versioning (workflows refined across multiple releases), logging, and ongoing support.
- **Documentation** — Build guides, setup docs, and configuration references produced alongside each system.
